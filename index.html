How do I Use BandStar?

That depends!

I'm An Audience Member

As an audience member, you simply download BandStar and run it. It will automatically tell you the name of the band at your location. If there's no band playing,then it will let you search for a nearby band.

I'm a Performer

As a performer, you use this site to setup your band, the venues in which you play, and the shows you will be performing at those venues.

I'm a Venue

As a venue, BandStar lets you advertise specials for tonight and attract both audiences and popular bands to your locale.

I Provide Software For Performers

BandStar provides opportunities for third-party integration. Out of the box, BandStar integrates with OnSong. If you have software that you think will integrate well, let us know!
